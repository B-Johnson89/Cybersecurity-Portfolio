# Real-World Case Studies (Sanitized)

This section contains three sanitized incident response investigations from Tier 1 service desk and identity support work at a Managed Services Provider (MSP) supporting multiple clients across higher education, medical device manufacturing, engineering, construction, and industrial sectors.

Each case study includes:

- A **My Role** section describing the specific Tier 1 contribution to the broader response
- **Investigation Sequence** showing the order of events and decision points
- **Analyst Notes** capturing the reasoning behind investigation priorities and pattern recognition
- **MITRE ATT&CK Mapping** for the techniques observed
- **Containment & Recovery Support** documenting the actions executed or coordinated
- **Lessons Learned** specific to the incident
- **Improvement Recommendations** that I would propose if contributing to a post-incident review

## Sanitization

All case studies have been fully sanitized: client names, ticket numbers, dates, internal identifiers, screenshots with sensitive data, and proprietary details have been removed or generalized. Containment and remediation actions described were executed in coordination with the client security team; investigation scope, decisions, and analyst reasoning reflect my Tier 1 contribution to the broader response.
